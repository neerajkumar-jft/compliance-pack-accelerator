Compliance Pack Accelerator

Databricks-native multi-jurisdiction compliance — pluggable regulation packs (DPDP India 2023 · UK GDPR · EU GDPR · CCPA · PIPEDA · …)
ADR-0001 · Per-data-subject routing
Architectural foundation. Compliance is per–data-subject, not per-deployment. Every customer-level row carries a jurisdiction column; rules apply per principal through the regulation pack governing them. An Indian SaaS with UK clients applies DPDP rules to Indian principals (730-day retention) and UK GDPR rules to UK principals (90-day retention) — same database, different rules per row, no manual sorting. Cross-cutting company-level obligations (breach notification, DPIA filing, DPO requirement) apply as the union of all loaded packs whose principals are present. Full rationale and 24 enumerated edge cases: ADR-0001.

Per-data-subject routing — the core architectural primitive.
jurisdiction = 'IN' → DPDP rules · 730-day retention · DPDP §11/§12 DSR rights · 10 Indian-language notices
jurisdiction = 'GB' → UK GDPR rules · 90-day retention · UK GDPR Art. 15-22 DSR rights · en-GB notices
jurisdiction = 'EU' → EU GDPR rules · per-Member-State retention · Art. 15-22 + Art. 7 lawful-basis · 24-locale notices
jurisdiction = 'US' → CCPA/CPRA rules · 45-day DSR SLA · opt-out of sale/sharing + GPC · 12 California-prevalent languages
jurisdiction IS NULL → ADR-0001 Q3 unmapped bucket · "Unmapped principals" counter tile live on the CCO Executive Overview
Cross-cutting company-level rules (breach notification, DPIA filing, DPO requirement, Significant Data Fiduciary registration, audit-log retention) apply as the union of every pack whose principals are present in the deployment — a breach affecting both IN and GB principals notifies both DPBI and ICO in parallel.

All packs in regulations/ load simultaneously; each customer-level row's jurisdiction column routes rule evaluation per-row. Adding UK GDPR / EU GDPR / CCPA / PIPEDA / LGPD is authoring 9 yaml files plus a pii_patterns.py + dpia_template.yaml — zero core code change. Same lakehouse + same personas + same dashboards, multiple regulations active per row.
