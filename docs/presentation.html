The DPDP Problem

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 exposes every Indian enterprise to material regulatory risk.
₹250 Cr

per contravention

Penalty for non-compliance with consent, rights, or security obligations

72 Hours

breach notification

Clock starts whether or not the organization knows who is affected

Annual DPIA

for Significant Data Fiduciaries

Must reflect current processing activities, not a 6-month-old snapshot

The operational reality today:

  • Personal data sits across 40+ source systems — no single register exists
  • Consent is captured as a Terms & Conditions acceptance that predates the Act's specificity requirement
  • Data subject rights are handled manually through legal inboxes with multi-week SLAs
  • No organization can reproduce historical processing decisions under regulator inspection

What Most Enterprises Look Like Today

Can't Find Their PII

Personal data across CRM, HRMS, databases, SaaS apps, file shares — no unified inventory. Compliance team doesn't know what they hold.

Consent Is a Checkbox

Legacy T&C acceptance predates DPDP's specificity requirement. No per-purpose granularity. No withdrawal mechanism. No evidence trail.

No Audit Trail

Cannot reproduce historical decisions under regulator inspection. No evidence of when consent was given or revoked. No time-travel capability.

Manual Everything

DSRs via legal inbox (weeks). DPIA as a document project (months). Compliance reporting as a quarterly fire drill. No automation.

Expansion Path — What Comes Next

The architecture is designed to grow. Each phase adds capabilities without re-architecture.
PHASE 1

Connect Real Data

Connect client's actual CRM/HRMS via Lakehouse Federation or Lakeflow Connect. Re-run PII scan against real data. Immediate compliance visibility.

PHASE 2

Data Subject Rights Hub

DSR portal (Databricks App). UC lineage-based discovery. Delta DELETE + VACUUM erasure. Erasure certificate generation.

PHASE 3

Breach Detection

Lakewatch agentic SIEM (post Private Preview). 72-hour alerting workflow. Agent Bricks DPBI notification drafting.

PHASE 4

Retention & Scale

Lakebase for consent OLTP. Crypto-shredding for backups. Cross-border transfer monitoring. Full six-module platform.
